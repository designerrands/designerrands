Instructions to report your design errands

Upload a photo of your design observation and share it with us. You will contribute to a database of design errands all around the world.

Some prompts to think about for that 280 characters:

  • How would an alien describe this?
  • What would a sensible designer do in this case?
  • If you can invent anything that can solve this problem, what would it be?
  • Imagine the kind of mishaps this can cause. Tell a story from it.
