No promises. Guaranteed.

Chapter 3: Honesty
Bellinzona, Mar 4, 2015. Image credit: RC.

The billboard says it, honestly: “0% promises. 100% engagement.” Honesty at its best. At this point, we do not even want to know what the honest candidate will do. We are supposed to trust him on account of the fact that he is making no promise, probably suggesting that if he made a promise, he will not be able to keep it.
