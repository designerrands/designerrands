Reappropriation is the name of the game

Place d’Italie, Paris, 9 april 2018. Image credit: RC.

In Place d’italie in Paris the upcoming Spring is anticipated by a bunch of inflatable implements that are tied to the grids protecting the ventilation openings of the underlying Metro station. They both decorate the passageway and hint at the unpleasant feeling one may get by walking over the grid.

By the same principle, here is something that could be used as inflatable shelter in case of rain.
