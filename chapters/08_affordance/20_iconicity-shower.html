Iconicity in the shower

Again on iconicity: how long before this icon will be read: “feel free to use your mobile while showering”? As the old soap bar is increasingly disappearing, replaced by shower gels, will it survive as an icon? Probably not as its shape is not distinctive enough to become iconic.
