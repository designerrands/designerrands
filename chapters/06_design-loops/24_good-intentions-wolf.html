Paris Metro, July 2nd, 2018. Image credit: RC.

Sexual harassment occurs in public spaces, in trains and subways, where it is easy to make contact and difficult to disentangle. French authorities warned of the risks and encouraged victims to report misconduct.

But isn’t the image sending the wrong message to the wrongdoer? A wolf, a predator, a true alpha male, hunting in the woods the lone prey.
