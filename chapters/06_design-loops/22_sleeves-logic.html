Sleeves and logic

Bastia, Jul 18, 2018. Image credit: RC

Pictures can express quantification. The picture on the right does not tell you that you should enter the office precisely in that attire, but that the length of your sleeves, pants and skirt should be at least that shown in the picture; and the picture on the left says that the length it depicts is not enough. (Picture logic has many other twists, and no-nonsense interpretation is important to sort them out: you do not take the pictures to accurately represent body shapes or skin color or the identity of individuals.)
