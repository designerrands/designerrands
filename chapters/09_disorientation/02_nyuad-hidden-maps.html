On the maps at NYU Abu Dhabi campus: the hidden maps

Maps have been of course one of the hot topics of the classes on Wayfinding. Students were quick to pick up areas of improvements in their design and placement: one orientation map on our campus is completely covered by carefully trimmed vegetation. We must say we take the side of the gardener here because that is an oriented map placed on a wrong wall and does not really provide any helpful information to the reader. Perhaps it is a good thing that it is impossible to access.
